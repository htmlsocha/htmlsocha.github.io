What is the project about?



It is a compass-like device, which was created as a form of decision-making game. With a compass hand you can pick symbols on outer circle that describe the situation you are dealing with. The answear will appear on the inner circle. The answear will be presented through six categories, that describe different parts of life: „Family”,„Material goods and body” , „Religion  and spirituality”, „Emotions”, „Law and society”, „Ambitions and work”

Mind Compass will show you which of those categories you should Focus on the most while solving the problem described by the symbols.
It accually helps our inner moral compass to make some choices.Thanks to being able to process the situation while picking up the symbols to describe it,the user is better prepared to make a decision. Then when algorythm calculates and gives them the answear, they can interpret it.



An example!

Lets say that your brother is sick but you really want to go to ascience fair. On one hand you should stay and watch over your family member, on the other-going there could be important for your education. You pick several symbols like: thunder for conflict, angel for guarding someone you love, money for possible benefits ineductation, bee for hard work, globe for education and discoveries and so on…

Then lets assume the compass pointed that you should consider family first, so it suggests that you should stay at home and watch after your sibling!
